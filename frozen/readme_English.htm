How to use

※Attention
If you get Abnormal End when you D&D vmd, load vmd file from upper menu "file"→"load motion data".

DL Okacchi's motion data from this URL,
http://www.nicovideo.jp/watch/sm24059084
and prepare wav file and your favorite model.

D&D all the pmx files in the pmx folder from 01 to 11, and brfore 11, load your model.

D&D four x files in the x_MME folder by this order.
1 ground1.x
2 ground2.x
3 AutoLuminous.x
4 wf_c4.x

Click "To Camera", and choose pmx and bone name in the accessory manipulation panel to three x files.
ground1.x → 02_dummy, ground1
ground2.x → 02_dummy, ground2
wf_c4.x → 06_castle, for_wf_c4

In the accessory manipulation panel, write in this way.

Click "background"→"accessories edit", and rewrite 1 to 2.

D&D the vmd files from 01 to 11 in the vmd folder, to the pmx files of same numbers.

D&D Okacchi's Let it go-motion.vmd to your model.

Click "To Camera", and D&D Okacchi's Let it go-camera.vmd, and D&D AdditionalCamera.vmd in the vmd folder.

In the light manipulation panel,rewrite R80 G80 B90 and click "register", and click "axis", and click "view"→"display ground shadow"(uncheck).

Load wav file and play！

If you use EnglishLip.vmd, first delete original "aiueo" and load EnglishLip.vmd after.
